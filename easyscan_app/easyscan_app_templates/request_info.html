easyScan info

This service permits Brown community members to request scans of up to 20 pages from non-special-collections materials located at the Annex.

Other info....

Eventually this service will be hooked up to Brown's VuFind and Blacklight discovery web-apps, but for now, to use this service:

  • Find an item you need a scan from in Josiah. Sample Josiah item.
  • Click the 'Request Scan' link next to the item from which you need a scan.
  • Log in.
  • Edit the citation information as needed.
  • Click the Submit button; that's it!

Your scan will be emailed to you with x business days.
