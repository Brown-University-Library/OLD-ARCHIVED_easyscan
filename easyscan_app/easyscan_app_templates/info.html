Important Notice

We are increasing our scanning services.

We will conduct scans and deliver them to your email address in the order in which they are received, in as timely a manner as possible.

We are assessing Library services on an ongoing basis. Check the Library's COVID-19 announcements page frequently for updates on Library services.

(On occasion you may see a 'scan' link next to a non-print item. If you attempt to request such a scan, the Annex staff will email you and remind you that this scanning service is only for print materials.)

In most cases material is scanned within 2 business days. Notification will be sent to your Brown email address.

Other info....

Eventually this service will be hooked up to Brown's other discovery services, but for now, to use this service:

  • Find an item from which you need a scan in Josiah. Sample item
  • Click the 'scan' link next to the item.
  • Log in.
  • Add citation/page-range information as needed.
  • Click the Submit button; that's it!
  • You'll see a confirmation web-page, and a confirmation email will be sent to you.

If you have questions, feel free to email {{ email_general_help|urlize }} or call {{ phone_general_help }}.
