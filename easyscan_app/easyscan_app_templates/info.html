easyScan info (under construction)

This service permits scans of up to 50 pages, or one chapter, from non-special-collections materials located at the Annex (Brown's offsite Library storage facility).

In most cases material is scanned within 2 business days. Notification will be sent to your brown email address.

Other info....

Eventually this service will be hooked up to Brown's VuFind and coming Blacklight discovery web-apps, but for now, to use this service:

  • Find an item you need a scan from in Josiah. Sample item on the Josiah test-server (VPN may be required).
  • Click the 'Request Scan' link next to the item from which you need a scan.
  • Log in.
  • Add citation/page-range information as needed.
  • Click the Submit button; that's it!
